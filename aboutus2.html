About Our Club

Adventure Club - UoK is the most organized adventure society of the University of Kelaniya. Throughout its history, it has effectively contributed to creating environmental awareness, health, and safety among university students and the general community. The club operates beyond any borders. Students from all the faculties, ethnic groups doing events and programs together. It is more than just a club. It is a family.

Adventure Club - UoK the only students’ association offering lifetime membership for students. Any member of the club can come back to the club at any time and do adventurers with the current students of the club. However, the membership procedure is very different than any other society. Participants are evaluated in a critical manner. Often behavior, environmental conservationism, the spirit of being a family are common determinants of membership evaluation. But no gender, race, or any other discriminating properties of the participants are valid in the evaluation.

 

Our History

Adventure Club – UoK is established in 2019 and registered in 2020 by few undergraduates representing all the faculties. The club has developed itself to be more environmentally conservative and wildlife protection while conduction adventure activities. It developed explicit regulations for its club culture under a solid constitution. More than just doing adventure activities, it has developed itself to conduct well-organized programs related to adventure activities with a wide scope. Likewise, it has grown stronger and stronger every day.
