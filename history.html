Our History

The initial idea for the Adventure Club was generated in late 2019 when our co-founders came together one day at the F4 building in the main university premises. From there they recruited more like-minded undergraduates from all six faculties of the University of Kelaniya. And they were able to hold our inaugural Anual General Meeting (AGM) on the 28th of October 2020 and officially became a registered club in the university.

As of today, we have done many projects like PROJECT INSPIRO to educate our adventurers on unique but Adventure-related topics like Environmental and wildlife conservation, safety, First-aid, photography but also topics like personal branding and LinkedIn management to help our members reach new heights in their personal and academic lives.
